# DRAFT BLOG POST: Why You Need to Stop Using VPNs in 2026

VPNs are officially dead technology. If you are paying for a consumer VPN service to "stay secure" on public Wi-Fi, you are wasting your money.

Thanks to the ubiquitous adoption of HTTPS, TLS 1.3, and DNS-over-HTTPS (DoH), your traffic is already encrypted end-to-end. A hacker sitting in a coffee shop can no longer see your passwords or read your emails; all they see is encrypted garbage. Furthermore, consumer VPNs actually *introduce* risk because you are routing all your traffic through a centralized, opaque third-party server that likely logs your data and sells it to advertisers.

Stop using VPNs. Rely on your browser's native encryption protocols, enable multi-factor authentication, and you will be perfectly safe on any network.
